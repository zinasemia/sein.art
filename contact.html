    Hi to whoever it may concern!

    If you are interested in this exhibition and you would like to ask us questions or participate, write to this mail: sein.exhibition@gmail.com .

    How to become a part of us:

    In the case that you would like to become an exhibiting Artist, and be a part of SEIN. exhibition, then please write us and include the following:

    • Your name and pronouns (if you want to share) or any other information that you think we should know!
    • What kind of art you would like to exhibit (e.g. photography, painting, performance, film, etc.)
    • If you would like to be on the mailing list, so that we can send you updates and dates for meetings!
    • And also feel free to ask at the Facebook group (sein. Intern) to become a member.

    We would like to point out that only people who identify themselves as FLINTA* can exhibit at this exhibition. Everyone else is more than welcome to visit, duhh.

    We are looking forward to your message!
